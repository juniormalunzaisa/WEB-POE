Our Story

Cloud Cakes began in a tiny Cape Town kitchen where late-night baking turned passion into perfection. From fluffy sponge cakes to iconic layered desserts, every creation carries warmth, joy, and a hint of magic.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Craft desserts that feel like memories — combining creativity and care with locally sourced ingredients.

Vision: To become a household name for joy, creativity, and connection through cakes that bring people together.
